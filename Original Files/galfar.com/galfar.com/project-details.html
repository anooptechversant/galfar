Bank Muscat HQ

Services works for the Main Office Building, Energy Center Building, and External Car Parking.

The total built-up area of the Main office Building is 36,000 Sqm with a Basement Floor + Ground Floor + 4 Upper Floors. The Footprint (Basement Floor) of the Building is 5,000 Sqm (approx) mainly utilized for Services facilities.

The Ground Floor is designed as a public area, which includes amenities like a Gymnasium, Coffee shops, Restaurants, Auditorium, Departmental store, and a branch of the Bank.

The upper floors consist of offices, which include an executive floor for Chairman, Chief executive officer, and Chief operating officer. The topmost floor is the IT Hub.

The Energy Center is a separate building that houses the Generators, chiller plants, Transformers& panels, water tank, and pumping system. The car park which has parking space for 1000 cars is made of interlock pavement, some area has a canopy with elegant synthetic fiber.
