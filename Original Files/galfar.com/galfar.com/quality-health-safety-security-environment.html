QUALITY, HEALTH, SAFETY, SECURITY AND ENVIRONMENT -SYSTEMS UNIT

Quality, Health, Safety, Security and Environment, these five components combine together to form a corporate management approach to bring in efficiency in all business processes leading to reduction in process errors, reworks, workplace accidents, occupational illness, protection of project assets and environmental impacts.

QHSSE Systems Unit functions integral with other Units of Galfar to facilitate continual improvement through Risk Assessments, Corporate QHSSE Plans, Management System Audits, Staff Competence Development, Monitoring Compliance and Carryout Management Reviews.

QHSE Systems Unit spearheads effective implementation of Quality and HSSE Management Systems by fostering Lean Management initiatives, Flawless Project Delivery Plans, Task Workshops and Trainings, Campaigns, Reporting of Quality and Safety Observations, Routine and Periodic Inspections, Incident Investigations, Learnings from Incidents and Corrective and preventive actions.

QHSSE Policies, System Procedures and other supportive QHSSE procedures and plans establishes and maintains a dynamic framework that drives the organization to Business Excellence.

QHSSE Management System of Galfar is certified to latest International Standards for Quality and HSSE Management System – ISO 9001, ISO/TS 29001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 which demonstrates our capability to meet with statutory body, Customer, Legal and other Interested Party requirements consistently.

Quality Policy

Galfar Engineering & Contracting SAOG envisions being the most preferred construction Company by conducting business with a strong focus on sustainable development through Quality and Reliability of our products and services.

We are committed to:

  • Consistently provide products and services to the satisfaction of the customer.
  • Comply with the applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
  • Meet requirements of the interested parties relevant to our Quality Management System.
  • Review and continually improve the Quality Management System.

To achieve this:

  • Identify and ensure needs and expectations of the Customer and other Interested Parties relevant to the Quality Management System.
  • Establish and review objectives and targets at all levels of the organization.
  • Identify and address Risks and Opportunities at different levels of business processes.
  • Collaborate with vendors for efficient external provision of materials and services.
  • Ensuring that employees are competent, empowered and aware of the applicable procedures and practices to be complied with.
  • Improve operational efficiency through Lean Principles.
  • Provide adequate work environment and infrastructure.
  • Verify compliance with contract specifications, standards, applicable statutory and regulatory requirements and procedures of the Company.
  • Continuously monitor and review business processes to ensure achievement of Objectives and Targets and take appropriate corrective measures where required.
  • Take continual improvement actions based on results of review and analysis.
  • This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure that the policy and management system is effective and appropriate.

HSSE Policy

Galfar Engineering & Contracting SAOG envisions being the most preferred construction Company by conducting business with a strong focus on sustainable development through effective implementation of Health, Safety, Security and Environment Management Systems complying with applicable legal requirements and International Standards.

We are committed to:

  • Being proactive in keeping people healthy, safe from harm and preserving environment.
  • Perform all works in compliance with (a) legal obligations, (b) contractual agreements, (c) internal procedures and, (d) applicable codes, standards and regulations.
  • Empower people to raise occupational health, safety and environmental concerns and as appropriate stop unsafe work conditions.
  • Promote consultation and participation of workers in developing HSSE programs and performance evaluation.
  • Provide appropriate and adequate resources for effective implementation of HSSE Management Systems
  • Elimination of Hazard and Minimizing the Occupational Health, Safety and Security Risks.
  • Reduce consumption of resources, generation of wastes, prevent pollution and protect the environment from the impact of activities carried out.
  • Share lessons learned and best practices with the goal of continual improvement of Health, Safety, Security and Environmental performance.

To achieve this:

  • Mandate company leaders at all levels to demonstrate visible commitment to Health, Safety and Environment protection and that HSSE is embedded in all our decisions and actions.
  • Establish objectives, targets, limits at appropriate levels and frequencies, HSE programs to continually improve HSSE performance.
  • Ensure regular monitoring, review of HSSE Management Systems and performance.
  • Ensure regular monitoring, review of HSSE Management Systems and performance.
  • Be supportive to the communities that host our projects, offices and use inclusive, participatory engagement by which local cultures and values are respected, dialogue is promoted and mutual trust is built.
  • Strengthen sustainability through education, innovation, and the appropriate use of relevant industry standards.
  • This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure that the policy and management system is effective and appropriate.

Drugs and Alcohol Policy

Galfar Engineering & Contracting SAOG, promotes drugs and alcohol-free environment for the safety and health of our employees and extended workforce. The company is committed to the elimination of drug and alcohol use and abuse in the workplace.

This policy does not prohibit employees from the lawful use and possession of prescribed medications. Employees must, however, consult with their doctors about the medications’ effect on their fitness for duty and ability to work safely, and they must promptly disclose any work restrictions to their supervisor or line manager.

To achieve this:

  • Company authorizes surprise checks, random and periodic alcohol breath tests in its worksites, offices, accommodations and other facilities to verify conformance to this policy.
  • Employees to be subjected for alcohol test when they cause or contribute to accidents that seriously damage Company assets or that result in an injury to themselves or another employee during working or non-working hours.
  • Working or driving at work under the influence of drugs or alcohol will lead to immediate dismissal.
  • Employees who refuse to cooperate in required tests or who use, possess, buy, sell, manufacture or dispense any illegal drugs or alcohol in violation of this policy shall be disciplined in line with company HR Manual and Oman legal rules.
  • Smoking is not allowed in any of our worksites, offices and living accommodations, apart from designated areas earmarked for the same. Smoking in company owned vehicles and equipment is strictly prohibited.

  • This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure that the policy and management system is effective and appropriate.

Road Safety Policy

Galfar Engineering & Contracting SAOG recognizes the impact of Road Traffic Accidents in our business and community in which we operate. We are committed to reduce such impacts through appropriate control measures and continual communication to all our employees and stakeholders.

We are committed to:

  • Comply with the contract requirements of our customers, applicable legal requirements of the country in which we operate.
  • Provide drivers and operators with necessary information, training, responsibility and accountability to ensure safe performance.
  • Emphasize good behavior, attitude and culture of drivers, operators, passengers and other road users as factors that can influence Road Safety.
  • Provide In-Vehicle Monitoring System (IVMS) in our operational vehicles considering the risks involved, as a tool to analyze and implement actions to promote safe driving behavior.
  • Conform to the requirements on load safety and restraining.
  • Focus continuously on optimizing kilometers driven, thus minimizing the road exposure.
  • Plan and schedule realistic driving hours to follow-up and complete journeys safely

To achieve this:

  • Deploy vehicles and equipment that are suitable to the requirement and affirmation of their safe working condition.
  • Adhere to the maintenance standards stipulated by the vehicle and equipment manufacturers.
  • Initiate disciplinary measures against non-compliance and violators of this policy. Driving company vehicles under the influence of drugs or alcohol will lead to immediate dismissal.
  • This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure that the policy and management system is effective and appropriate.
